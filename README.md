# DeepThought Federer Company Research

## Objective
This project identifies and evaluates Hyderabad-based specialty manufacturing and deep-tech companies that fit DeepThought’s “Federer Company” ICP framework.

## Focus Areas
- Aerospace & Precision Engineering
- Industrial IoT & Automation
- Specialty Manufacturing
- Embedded Systems & Sensors

## Deliverables
- 25 researched companies
- Federer scoring framework
- Research methodology
- Scaling proposal for identifying 1000 ICP-qualified companies

## City Chosen
Hyderabad

## Segments Chosen
- Industrial Sensors & Instrumentation
- Precision Engineering & Aerospace
