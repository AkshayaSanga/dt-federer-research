# 1000 Company Scaling Proposal

## Objective
Build a verified list of 1000 ICP-qualified companies within one month.

## Sources
- DSIR R&D lists
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Industry expos
- BSE SME listings
- MCA filings
- Manufacturing directories

## Workflow

Universe of Companies
↓
Initial Filtering
↓
AI-Assisted Scoring
↓
Manual Verification
↓
Quality Check
↓
Final ICP List

## Automation
Automation would be implemented using:
- Python scraping
- AI-assisted scoring
- Data enrichment APIs
- Spreadsheet validation

## Quality Control
- Remove distributors and service companies
- Verify manufacturing evidence
- Verify growth signals
- Check ownership structure
- Verify operational maturity

## Weekly Plan

### Week 1
Build company universe

### Week 2
Scraping and scoring

### Week 3
Manual verification

### Week 4
Final validation and personalization
