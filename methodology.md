# Methodology

## Research Process

The research process followed a structured filtering and evaluation methodology based on DeepThought’s Federer Company ICP framework.

### Step 1 — Initial Company Discovery
Companies were identified using:
- LinkedIn
- F6S startup database
- Company websites
- BSE filings
- Industry news articles
- Manufacturing directories

### Step 2 — Eligibility Filtering
Companies were filtered based on:
- Manufacturing capability
- Operational presence in India
- Founder-led or promoter-driven structure
- Specialty or differentiated products

### Step 3 — Federer Scoring
Each company was evaluated across:
- Differentiation
- Decision-maker quality
- Sector growth tailwinds
- Growth signals
- Systems maturity
- Leadership succession

### Step 4 — Validation
Evidence was collected from:
- Company websites
- Annual reports
- Regulatory certifications
- News articles
- Public filings

## Key Learnings
The strongest Hyderabad companies showed:
- Deep manufacturing capabilities
- Export-oriented growth
- Aerospace and defence tailwinds
- Strong operational systems
- Technical leadership teams
